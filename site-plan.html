Purpose

The‌ ‌goal‌ ‌of‌ ‌the‌ ‌bar‌ ‌is‌ ‌to‌ ‌create‌ ‌a‌ ‌welcoming‌ ‌and‌ ‌fun‌ ‌bar-like‌ ‌atmosphere‌ ‌while‌ ‌maintaining‌ ‌our‌ ‌ standards.‌ ‌The‌ ‌website‌ ‌I‌ ‌am‌ ‌creating‌ ‌will‌ ‌have‌ ‌details‌ ‌on‌ ‌the‌ ‌hours,‌ ‌the‌ ‌menu,‌ ‌specialty‌ ‌drinks,‌ ‌ and‌ ‌events.‌ ‌It‌ ‌will‌ ‌have‌ ‌details‌ ‌about‌ ‌each‌ ‌drink‌ ‌and‌ ‌its‌ ‌ingredients,‌ ‌so‌ ‌people‌ ‌can‌ ‌know‌ ‌what‌ ‌ they‌ ‌are‌ ‌going‌ ‌to‌ ‌be‌ ‌drinking.‌ ‌The‌ ‌goal‌ ‌of‌ ‌the‌ ‌bar‌ ‌is‌ ‌to‌ ‌have‌ ‌“Fun‌ ‌without‌ ‌the‌ ‌toxic.”‌ ‌A‌ ‌bar‌ ‌is‌ ‌a‌ ‌ place‌ ‌where‌ ‌u‌ ‌go‌ ‌with‌ ‌friends,‌ ‌have‌ ‌a‌ ‌drink,‌ ‌dance‌ ‌on‌ ‌the‌ ‌dance‌ ‌floor,‌ ‌have‌ ‌fun,‌ ‌and‌ ‌chill.‌ ‌Kas‌ ‌J‌ ‌ Bar‌ ‌provides‌ ‌a‌ ‌fun‌ ‌affordable‌ ‌place‌ ‌for‌ ‌students‌ ‌to‌ ‌go‌ ‌to‌ ‌at‌ ‌night‌ ‌that‌ ‌has‌ ‌something‌ ‌for‌ ‌ everyone‌ ‌and‌ ‌drinks‌ ‌which‌ ‌who‌ ‌doesn’t‌ ‌love‌ ‌that?‌

Audience

The typical audience will be college students around Rexburg, Idaho. The site will typically be accessed on mobile. The audience will be able to access the menu on the site. They will also be able to learn about operating hours and events.
